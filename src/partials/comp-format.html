Competition format

The league is played online between players accross the season, a total of 6 rounds, each round consists of 2 games. The final round of the season is also know as the 'Main Event' and double points are awarded to the winner of that round.

Three hundred points are awarded for first place, 200 for second place and 45 points for entry for each game, with the player with the most points at the end of the season winning the Montessori Poker League.
